Esports in League

Summary

This Website is a League of Legends Esports statistics site, where you can see the statistics of every single team and player!

We also want to introduce the major teams and tell you their history!

We also want to introduce you to the main champions of certain players with the rune set up and item builds!


Our goals

Because the esports industry is getting bigger every year, we want to introduce you to the biggest esport industry and tell you about its teams and players!

We also want you to replicate certain builds from pro players and try to keep it updated every patch!
